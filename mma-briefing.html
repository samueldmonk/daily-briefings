Top Story

Eight days out: UFC Freedom 250 takes Topuria–Gaethje to the White House lawn

The first professional sporting event ever staged at the presidential residence is nearly here. UFC Freedom 250 — tied to the United States' 250th anniversary — lands on the South Lawn of the White House on Sunday, June 14. Undisputed lightweight champion Ilia Topuria puts his title on the line against interim champ Justin Gaethje in a unification main event, while Alex Pereira faces Ciryl Gane for the interim heavyweight title in the co-main.

The seven-fight card also features Sean O'Malley vs. Aiemann Zahabi, Mauricio Ruffy vs. Michael Chandler, Bo Nickal vs. Kyle Daukaus and Diego Lopes vs. Steve Garcia, plus Derrick Lewis vs. Josh Hokit, per ESPN and Tapology listings. First, though, there's business tonight: UFC Fight Night caps the week at the APEX with Belal Muhammad facing Gabriel Bonfim at welterweight.
