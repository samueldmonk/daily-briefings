UFC FREEDOM 250 · JUNE 14

UFC goes to the White House: Topuria–Gaethje unification headlines first pro sporting event at the presidential residence

UFC Freedom 250 lands on the White House grounds June 14 — the first professional sporting event ever staged at the presidential residence, timed to Flag Day. Reigning lightweight champ Ilia Topuria meets interim champ Justin Gaethje to unify the belts, while former two-division king Alex Pereira makes his heavyweight debut in the co-main, chasing the interim title. Ten days out, this is the biggest spectacle on the 2026 calendar.
