Top Story

Topuria dethroned and injured: orbital fractures in both eyes

Justin Gaethje pulled off a stunning upset of Ilia Topuria via a fourth-round TKO (corner stoppage) at UFC Freedom 250 — the promotion's first event on the White House grounds, on June 14 — to claim the undisputed lightweight title. It was the first defeat of Topuria's professional career, dropping him to 17-1.

Topuria came out of the fight with orbital fractures in both eyes and was handed a six-month (180-day) medical suspension; he is expected to be sidelined for the rest of 2026. Gaethje now anchors a reshuffled lightweight division as its undisputed champion.

Business & broadcast. UFC Freedom 250 (June 14) was the first UFC event staged on the White House grounds.
