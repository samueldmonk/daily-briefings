Top Story

Fight week: Rafael Fiziev meets Manuel Torres atop UFC Baku on Saturday

The UFC returns to Azerbaijan this Saturday, June 27 — its second trip to Baku, after Hill vs. Rountree Jr. in June 2025 — with a lightweight main event between Rafael Fiziev and 15th-ranked Manuel Torres at the National Gymnastics Arena in Baku. Indicative betting has Fiziev a −145 favorite over Torres at +125 (SportsLine), with DraftKings listing Fiziev around −115 — a close, high-stakes striking matchup near the top of the 155-pound contender pool. The main card streams on Paramount+, with prelims around midday ET.

The card carries several names to watch beneath the headliner, including a 185-pound bout between Shara Magomedov and Michel Pereira and a lightweight pairing of Nazim Sadykhov and Matheus Camilo. It follows Friday's UFC Vegas 119, where Manel Kape avenged a prior loss to Kyoji Horiguchi and strengthened his case for a flyweight title shot.

Prospect Watch

Murtazali Magomedov Prospect

The featherweight announced himself at UFC Vegas 119 with what Yahoo Sports called the fastest twister submission in UFC history — finishing Melsik Baghdasaryan in 1:17 of Round 1 and banking a $100,000 Performance of the Night bonus.

Ateba Gautier Prospect

The Cameroonian middleweight (11–1) is 2–0 in 2026 — a decision over Andrey Pulyaev at UFC 324 (Jan 24) and a second-round TKO of Ozzy Diaz at UFC 328 (May 9) — and remains one of the division's fastest risers.

Around the Sport
  • Kape's title push. His revenge TKO of Horiguchi — a fourth straight finish — vaulted him into the top three of the flyweight rankings, behind only champion Joshua Van and former champ Alexandre Pantoja.

Rankings & Business

Rankings movement: Manel Kape pushes toward a flyweight title shot with a fourth consecutive finish, while newcomer Murtazali Magomedov makes an immediate statement with a record-fast twister at featherweight.

Business & broadcast: The UFC's U.S. home is Paramount+ — its ESPN deal ended at the close of 2025 — and UFC Baku plus the upcoming Fight Nights stream there. The promotion remains a unit of TKO Group Holdings.
