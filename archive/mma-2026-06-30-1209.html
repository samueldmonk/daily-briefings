Top Story
Fiziev Delivers Emotional Homecoming KO at UFC Baku
Rafael Fiziev captured a spectacular second-round stoppage over Manuel Torres at UFC Fight Night in Baku on June 27, wobbling Torres with a spinning wheel kick before swarming to finish at 0:15 of Round 2. The win marked an emotional homecoming for the Azerbaijani-based lightweight and punctuated a dominant card featuring four performance bonuses distributed to Fiziev, Asu Almabayev, Abdul-Rakhman Yakhyaev, and Daniil Donchenko ($100K each). Nine finishes overall, four of them submissions.
