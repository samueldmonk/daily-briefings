Top Story

Fiziev's Baku Masterclass: Spinning Wheel Kick TKO Puts Lightweight Elite on Notice

Rafael Fiziev delivered one of June's most decisive performances at UFC Baku on June 27, connecting on a stunning spinning wheel kick mid-fight to stun Manuel Torres before finishing with follow-up strikes in the second round. The finish elevates Fiziev into the periphery of the title conversation at 155 lbs, where undisputed champion Justin Gaethje (who claimed the belt at Freedom 250 on June 14) and a deep challenger pool continue to reshape the division.
