Top Story

Ankalaev–Guskov headlines Abu Dhabi Saturday: a −500 favorite meets a 100%-finish-rate spoiler

UFC Fight Night: Ankalaev vs Guskov lands Saturday at Etihad Arena in Abu Dhabi — a 13-fight card with prelims at 9 AM ET and the main card at noon ET on Paramount+ (UFC.com/DraftKings Network). Former light heavyweight champion Magomed Ankalaev (21-2-1, 1 NC) was originally slated to face Khalil Rountree Jr., who withdrew with an injury; No. 9-ranked Bogdan Guskov (18-3-1) stepped in on less than two weeks' notice.

The books see it lopsided: Ankalaev is −500 with Guskov +340 at FanDuel, while UFC's official odds board shows −550/+400 (Yahoo Sports/khelnow). Guskov's path to the upset is the one he always takes — every one of his wins has come by finish. Ceremonial weigh-ins stream Friday, Jul 24 on UFC's YouTube, Kick, TikTok, Facebook and Instagram channels (DraftKings Network).
