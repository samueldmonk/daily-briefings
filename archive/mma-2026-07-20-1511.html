Top Story

Volkan Oezdemir accepts 16-month EPO ban — positive in both blood and urine

Per a statement from the UFC's Combat Sports Anti-Doping programme, the Swiss light heavyweight tested positive for EPO — a substance in the Peptide Hormones and Growth Factors class — detected in both urine and blood from out-of-competition samples collected on February 16, 2026 and March 5, 2026 in Le Mouret, Switzerland.

The period of ineligibility runs from February 16, 2026, making him eligible again on June 17, 2027. The sanction was reduced to 16 months on the finding that Oezdemir did not seek out the substance and was transparent throughout: he was approached unsolicited by a local pharmacist following his November 2025 bout.

Oezdemir is accurately described as a former title challenger — he fought for the light heavyweight championship and lost — a descriptor confirmed by Yahoo Sports and Sherdog. He was never a champion, and this page does not call him one.

Around the Sport

  • Irene Aldana has retired at 38. The former title challenger finishes 15–8 overall and 8–5 in the UFC across roughly a decade on the roster. ESPN Deportes reported it first; Aldana then confirmed and released the letter she had sent Dana White back in April. The decision traces to her final appearance at Noche UFC 2024, a unanimous-decision loss to Norma Dumont in which she suffered one of the worst cuts in UFC history — an injury that has required multiple surgeries since. She is no longer eligible for a ranking and has been removed from the top 15.
  • Jon Jones remains unbooked. Dana White, asked in Oklahoma City: "That's up to him. He likes to talk about it, but actually getting a fight booked is a whole nother ball game." No booking is claimed here.
  • Zuffa Boxing 10: McKenna vs. Oliha is set for August 8 at the 3Arena in Dublin.
  • UFC BJJ 10: Tackett vs. Gracie goes Thursday, August 20 at 8:00 PM ET.
  • Bella Mir is expected to make her Dana White's Contender Series debut this summer. Framed as an expectation — no booking has been confirmed.
