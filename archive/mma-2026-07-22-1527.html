Top Story

Ankalaev headlines UFC Abu Dhabi on Saturday against late-notice Guskov

The UFC returns to the Etihad Arena in Abu Dhabi on Saturday, July 25 with a 13-fight card topped by a light-heavyweight main event: No. 2-ranked Magomed Ankalaev faces No. 9 Bogdan Guskov, who stepped in on short notice for the injured Khalil Rountree Jr. Ankalaev opened a heavy favorite at roughly -535, with Guskov around +400 (DraftKings). The six-bout main card is scheduled for approximately noon ET on Paramount+.

Around the Sport
  • Topuria sidelined: Ilia Topuria (17-1) was handed a 180-day medical suspension after losing the lightweight title to Justin Gaethje via fourth-round TKO at UFC Freedom 250 (June 14).

Rankings & Business

Rankings movement

Topuria and Pereira fell in the pound-for-pound rankings after their Freedom 250 stoppages. Ankalaev (No. 2) can bolster his light-heavyweight standing with a win over No. 9 Guskov on Saturday.

Business & broadcast

The UFC's seven-year Paramount+ arrangement has moved cards off traditional pay-per-view — recent and upcoming events, from Freedom 250 to Abu Dhabi and UFC 330, stream on Paramount+.
