Top Story

Fight day in Baku: Fiziev defends home turf against Torres as the prelims roll

Live Today

The UFC is back in Baku on Saturday, June 27, and it's a homecoming for Rafael Fiziev (13-5), who headlines the National Gymnastics Arena against Mexico's surging Manuel Torres (17-3) in a five-round lightweight main event. Fiziev needs the win badly, having dropped four of his last five — including a second-round knockout loss to Mauricio Ruffy in February — while Torres arrives as a finisher on the rise. The bookmakers can't separate them: it's a near pick-'em, with Torres a razor-thin favorite. The prelims have wrapped and the main card is underway (noon ET, Paramount+); the Fiziev–Torres headliner has not yet taken place.

On the prelims, Baku's Tahir Abdullayev stopped Brazil's unbeaten Jefferson Nascimento by third-round TKO (ground-and-pound, 4:28) in front of the home crowd; The Ultimate Fighter Season 33 winner Daniil Donchenko flattened short-notice newcomer Theodor Berggren with a second-round head-kick TKO (1:35); and Brazil's Jean Matsumoto edged Kazakhstan's Bekzat Almakhan by unanimous decision (29-28 across all three cards) in a back-and-forth bantamweight scrap. Full main-card results — Fiziev vs. Torres and the Shara Magomedov–Michel Pereira co-main — will post once the card concludes this afternoon.
