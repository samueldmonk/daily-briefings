McGregor returns: Conor McGregor vs. Max Holloway 2 set for UFC 329 on July 11

Conor McGregor is booked to headline UFC 329 on Saturday, July 11 at T-Mobile Arena in Las Vegas — the centerpiece of International Fight Week — rematching Max Holloway in a welterweight bout. It will be McGregor's first appearance since he broke his leg at UFC 264 in July 2021, a nearly five-year absence, and marks Holloway's debut at welterweight. The two first met back in August 2013 as featherweights, a fight McGregor won by unanimous decision. The main card also features a ranked lightweight clash between #5 Benoît Saint Denis and #6 Paddy Pimblett, streaming on Paramount+ with no pay-per-view.
