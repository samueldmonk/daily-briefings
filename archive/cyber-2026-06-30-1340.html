Kodak Breach Confirmed: 2.2M Records Stolen by ShinyHunters

Jun 18, 2026
ShinyHunters listed Kodak on its dark web extortion site Jun 15 with 2.2M customer PII and internal corporate data; gave 3-day ultimatum. Kodak confirmed the breach Jun 18 (deadline day, no payment disclosed) but did not detail which customer categories or how many were affected. Investigators engaged; no indication of how the group gained initial access. ShinyHunters has claimed 100+ breaches in 2026 alone, including Charter (42M), Oracle PeopleSoft (100+ companies), and Instructure Canvas (9,000+ schools).

THREAT ACTOR SPOTLIGHT

ShinyHunters — 100+ Breaches in 2026

Highly organized extortion crew operating since 2024; 2026 marked a massive acceleration. Known victims: Charter Communications (42M records, stolen via PeopleSoft zero-day), Oracle PeopleSoft (100+ companies), Instructure Canvas (9,000+ schools), Baker Distributing (Salesforce), Kodak, and dozens of healthcare/finance targets. Tactics: initial access via SaaS platform zero-days (PeopleSoft, Salesforce, Canvas), dark-web extortion with 3–7 day deadlines, data publication if ransom unpaid. Infrastructure: tracked to Russian/Eastern European nexus. Defense: patch SaaS platforms aggressively; monitor lateral movement from cloud-to-on-prem.
