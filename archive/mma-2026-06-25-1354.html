Top Story

McGregor returns: UFC 329 set for July 11 in Las Vegas

Conor McGregor is booked to headline UFC 329: McGregor vs. Holloway 2 on July 11 at T-Mobile Arena in Paradise, Nevada, the centerpiece of the promotion's International Fight Week. The welterweight bout marks McGregor's first walk to the Octagon since he broke his leg at UFC 264 in July 2021 — and it doubles as Max Holloway's welterweight debut, a rematch of their 2013 featherweight meeting (won by McGregor via decision). In the co-main event, Paddy Pimblett meets the heavy-handed Benoit Saint-Denis, and the card streams on Paramount+. Oddsmakers make McGregor an underdog — BETOnline lists him at +215 to Holloway's −255 — but it is shaping up as the year's marquee box-office draw.

Navajo Stirling prospect
