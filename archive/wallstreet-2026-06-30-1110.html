THE LEAD — as of ~11 AM ET

Markets Navigate Mixed Signals as Tech Stabilizes

The S&P 500 edged up 0.03% in midday trading while the Nasdaq Composite posted a 0.29% gain, suggesting a recovery in technology shares after recent volatility. The Dow retreated 0.24%, reflecting weakness in industrials and defense stocks. Intraday breadth suggests a risk-on shift driven by semiconductor strength and renewed optimism over easing Middle East tensions. Oil rallied 1.3% to $70.17/bbl on geopolitical relief; the 10-year Treasury yield settled at 4.376%.
