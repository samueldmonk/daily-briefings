Top Story

The Notorious returns: McGregor–Holloway 2 headlines International Fight Week

The fight world's attention turns to Las Vegas as UFC 329 (Saturday, July 11, T-Mobile Arena) marks the return of former two-division champion Conor McGregor — his first bout in roughly five years — against fourth-ranked Max Holloway in a welterweight main event. It's a rematch of their 2013 featherweight fight, which McGregor won by decision. With the card order now finalized, Paddy Pimblett meets Benoit Saint Denis in the co-main event and Cory Sandhagen faces Mario Bautista on a stacked International Fight Week bill.

Oddsmakers install Holloway as the favorite given McGregor's long absence, though lines have drifted toward the returning Irishman as fight week approaches. The full card streams on Paramount+.
