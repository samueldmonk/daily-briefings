THE LEAD

As of ~10:35 AM ET Tuesday, June 30: The S&P 500 has climbed 0.03% to around 7,441; Nasdaq Composite up 0.29% near 25,825. The Dow Jones has slipped 0.24%, still hovering around 52,080 after Monday's record-high close (52,182). The Russell 2000 trades flat. Tech and communication services sectors are outperforming on hopes for lasting Middle East relief and continued AI-driven gains in the mega-cap cohort (Magnificent Seven). Energy and industrials lag on crude's slide toward $70/bbl and mixed earnings-season signals. The VIX has ticked up to 18.41, reflecting modest volatility expectations. The Fed held rates steady at 3.5%–3.75% on June 17; inflation remains elevated, and the Committee's latest projections hinted at a possible rate hike by year-end.
