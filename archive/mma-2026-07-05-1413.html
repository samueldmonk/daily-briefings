Top Story

Conor McGregor returns at UFC 329 to headline International Fight Week

Conor McGregor steps back into the Octagon on Saturday, July 11 at T-Mobile Arena in Las Vegas — his first fight since he broke his leg against Dustin Poirier at UFC 264 in July 2021. He meets former featherweight champion Max Holloway, who moves up to welterweight for the bout, in a rematch of their August 2013 featherweight meeting, which McGregor won by decision.

Oddsmakers see a very different fight this time: Holloway is installed the favorite at −240, with McGregor a +180 underdog after nearly five years away — though the line has already drifted McGregor's way from a −550/+420 open (OddsShark, this run). The main card is slated for 9 PM ET / 6 PM PT.
