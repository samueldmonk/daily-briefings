The Lead

Dow Breaks 52,000 as Tech Rallies; Alphabet Joins Index
Stocks surged on Monday, June 29, with the Dow closing at 52,182.74 (+0.59%), S&P 500 at 7,440.43 (+1.18%), and Nasdaq Composite at 25,820.14 (+2.07%) — all closing higher for the second straight session. The rally was driven by Alphabet's +4.96% surge following its addition to the Dow Jones Industrial Average (replacing Verizon), strong tech breadth (Tesla +8.45%, Amazon +3.18%, Meta +2.2%), and easing geopolitical tensions as the US and Iran agreed to halt strikes ahead of resumed peace talks on Tuesday. Rocket Lab announced an $8B acquisition of Iridium, with Rocket Lab up 15.9% and Iridium up 25.4%.
