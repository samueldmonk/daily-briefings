Top Story

Kape avenges a nine-year-old loss — and calls for the belt

At UFC Vegas 119 on Saturday (June 20, UFC APEX), Manel Kape weathered two competitive rounds against Kyoji Horiguchi before uncorking a flush right hand and closing the show with ground-and-pound for a third-round TKO at 2:42. The win avenges a 2017 RIZIN loss to Horiguchi and runs Kape's streak to four straight in a five-round flyweight contender eliminator.

Afterward, Kape argued he has done enough to challenge flyweight champion Joshua Van — "It was enough," he said — while signalling he's willing to wait if Van first runs back a rematch with former champ Alexandre Pantoja. Kape also banked a $100,000 Performance of the Night bonus, one of four six-figure awards on a finish-heavy card.

Business & broadcast. UFC Vegas 119 paid out four $100,000 bonuses — Performance nods to Kape and Magomedov, plus a Fight of the Night split between Oliveira and Fili — on a card streamed on Paramount+, now the UFC's exclusive U.S. home for Fight Night cards. The summer slate builds toward International Fight Week and McGregor's return at UFC 329 (July 11).
