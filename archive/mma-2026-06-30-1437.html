Fiziev's Homecoming Dominant — Opens July with Baku TKO

Rafael Fiziev delivered a highlight-reel knockout in front of his home crowd on June 27, 2026, at UFC Fight Night: Fiziev vs Torres in Baku, Azerbaijan. Fiziev wobbled Manuel Torres with a spinning wheel kick in the second round before swarming with ground strikes, forcing a stoppage at 0:15 of Round 2. The victory marks a statement performance as the calendar turns to July, positioning Fiziev among the elite lightweights and setting up potential title implications for the division.

Rankings & Business

Meta UFC Rankings Launch (Jun 20)

The UFC replaced its legacy media voting system with the Meta UFC Rankings on June 20, 2026. The new Elo-based mathematical model prioritizes objectivity by accounting for opponent quality, activity level, and decay for long layoffs or outdated wins. This shift aims to reduce subjectivity and improve accuracy in title-contention positioning.
