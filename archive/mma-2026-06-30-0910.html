Top Story

Fiziev Delivers Wheel-Kick Masterpiece in Baku Homecoming
Rafael Fiziev (13-5-0) capped an emotional homecoming performance at UFC Fight Night: Baku with a stunning second-round KO over Manuel Torres (17-3-0). Fiziev landed a perfectly-timed spinning wheel kick that sent Torres crashing to the canvas, then finished with ground strikes before the referee stopped the fight. The performance earned Fiziev a $100,000 Performance of the Night bonus and marks a signature moment in his quest for UFC gold at lightweight.
