TOP STORY
Critical libssh2 RCE (CVE-2026-55200) — PoC Live
A malicious SSH server can trigger memory corruption in libssh2 clients via crafted packets, leading to arbitrary code execution. CVSS 9.2; PoC published June 29 on GitHub. Affects curl, Git, PHP, and any application using libssh2 (affecting hundreds of millions of systems). No user interaction or credentials required — the flaw is in the client-side SSH handshake parser. Patch immediately: libssh2 commit 97acf3dfda80c91c3a8c9f2372546301d4a1a7a8.

THREAT ACTOR SPOTLIGHT
ShinyHunters — PeopleSoft Mass Compromise
The ShinyHunters cybercrime group claimed compromise of Oracle PeopleSoft servers at 100+ organizations (primarily colleges and universities). Victims include student records with names, home addresses, phone numbers, emails, dates of birth, ethnicity, enrollment status, GPAs, majors, and student IDs. Attack surface: misconfigured or unpatched PeopleSoft instances.

Breaches & Incidents

Klue Hit by Icarus Ransomware via OAuth Attack
An OAuth supply-chain attack orchestrated by the Icarus ransomware group compromised Klue, stealing Salesforce CRM data from downstream victims including HackerOne, Gong, OneTrust, Tanium, and Huntress. Exposed: customer names, business email addresses, phone numbers, job titles, sales notes, CRM records, pricing information, and internal sales communications.
NEW
Maine Public Data Breach Portal Offline After Fraudulent Posts
The State of Maine took its public data-breach reporting portal offline on June 13 after bad actors posted fraudulent breach disclosures. The state is re-examining access controls and posting procedures.

Ukrainian Conti Ransomware Operative Pleads Guilty
A Ukrainian national extradited to the United States pleaded guilty to conspiracy charges tied to the Conti ransomware operation. Sentencing pending.
