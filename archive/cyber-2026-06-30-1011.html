Top Story

Klue / Icarus Supply Chain Breach — OAuth Token Theft at Scale

On June 12, 2026, competitive intelligence firm Klue detected an intrusion spanning back to June 11. Attackers gained access via a compromised legacy credential in Klue's integration infrastructure, harvested OAuth tokens connected to Salesforce, and impersonated Klue within downstream customer CRM environments. Exfiltration affected approximately 200 organizations, including major cybersecurity vendors Huntress, Recorded Future, Tanium, Jamf, BeyondTrust, and LastPass. Icarus extortion group claimed responsibility on June 19 and issued a June 22 deadline for contact before releasing stolen data. Salesforce disabled Klue Battlecards integration on June 17; Klue revoked credentials and tokens and launched a comprehensive investigation.
