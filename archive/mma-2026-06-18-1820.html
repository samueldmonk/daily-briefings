Top Story

Gaethje crowns himself at the White House, banks $825K in record bonuses

Justin Gaethje is the new undisputed UFC lightweight champion after stopping Ilia Topuria via corner stoppage following the fourth round at UFC Freedom 250 on June 14 — handing Topuria the first loss of his MMA career (now 17-1). On a historic card where every fight ended in a knockout, the UFC raised its bonuses to $400,000 (Fight of the Night) and $425,000 (Performance of the Night); Gaethje swept both for a $825,000 haul, Topuria earned $400,000 for the Fight of the Night, and Ciryl Gane took the other $425,000 after a second-round knockout of Alex Pereira to claim the interim heavyweight title. Topuria has since said he lost vision in both eyes and wants a rematch, but Dana White says Topuria was hospitalized after the bout and has no plans to rebook him soon.
