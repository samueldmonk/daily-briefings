The Lead

Great rotation: Dow surges 900+ points to a record as Broadcom's plunge drags AI trade

The Dow rallied roughly 911 points (+1.8%) to a fresh record Thursday while the Nasdaq slipped, as investors rotated out of chip and AI names into banks, healthcare, and retail. The trigger: Broadcom cratered about 14% after a fiscal Q2 revenue miss, prompting a broad paring of AI exposure. Consumer-oriented stocks got an extra lift as oil and Treasury yields eased following a ceasefire agreement between Israel and Lebanon, dialing back the Middle East risk premium that had rattled markets Wednesday.
