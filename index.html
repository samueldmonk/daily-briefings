Security

Threat level: High

An automated "FortiBleed" campaign has harvested working credentials from ~86,600 internet-facing Fortinet firewalls across 194 countries — about half the FortiGates online — while the Klue/Icarus Salesforce extortion widens (LastPass plus 10+ firms) and Tata Electronics confirms a 200,000-file breach. The most urgent fix remains the overdue, actively-exploited Splunk RCE (CVE-2026-20253, CVSS 9.8), with a CISA KEV trio (Chromium V8, Cisco SD-WAN, Arista EOS) due today.
