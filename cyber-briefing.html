Charter Communications breach: ShinyHunters leaks 13M+ customer records on the dark web

Charter Communications, one of the largest US telecommunications providers, suffered a significant data breach after the ShinyHunters group leaked more than 13 million customer records. Exposed data includes full names, email addresses, company and home addresses, details from nearly 10 million customer support tickets, and records on roughly 27,000 employees including work emails and job titles. The leak adds Charter to a growing list of major telecom targets this year.
