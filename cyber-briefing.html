Top Story

ShinyHunters leaks 13 million Charter Communications customer records

Charter Communications, one of the largest US telecom providers, has suffered a major data breach after the ShinyHunters extortion group leaked more than 13 million customer records on the dark web. Exposed data includes full names, email addresses, physical addresses, and details pulled from customer support tickets — plus records on roughly 27,000 Charter employees. The leak lands amid a wave of early-June incidents hitting healthcare, staffing, technology, and textile firms.

Ransomware

Abyss group hits German county administration

The Limburg-Weilburg county administration in Germany was struck by the Abyss ransomware group, which claims to have stolen and encrypted around 132 GB of data. Zurich carpentry firm Schneebeli AG was separately hit by the AiLock group.

AI Threat

AI-built ransomware toolkit automates EDR evasion

A threat actor is using an AI-assisted ransomware toolkit that automates Active Directory discovery and endpoint-defense evasion. Payloads were developed with AI coding agents and tested against major EDR products in virtual environments.

"WeedHack" campaign infects 116,000+ Minecraft players
